Who are we?
We are a bank that was born in 1981 with a bold vision. From its humble beginnings, we have grown and evolved, becoming a reliable pillar for the community. With a unique focus on the customer and a commitment to innovation, we have overcome challenges in the ever-changing financial world. Today, we are leaders in integrity and excellence, contributing to economic development with services that strengthen individuals and businesses alike

Mission:
The mission of Unity Bank is to meet the banking and financial needs of the national market by intermediating between savers and borrowers, with the aim of generating benefits for customers, shareholders, and employees.
